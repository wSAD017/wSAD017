Types of Transport

Ground Transportation

This type of transport is made up of vehicles capable of moving overland.
This type of transport is made up of vehicles capable of moving overland. They can be equipped with a motor or with human or animal propulsion. This type of transport is the most used and is used to move around the city in daily life. On the other hand, at a commercial level, it is very useful for the transfer of merchandise between nearby cities. Also, given the infrastructure, it competes in costs with maritime transport in terms of countries on the same continent. It has great logistical flexibility with access to almost any point.

car - Carro

metro - metro

Marine Transport

This group is made up of vehicles that move through the sea. Both on the surface and below it.
Likewise, at a commercial level, it is the international freight transport par excellence. This, due to its low cost and high capacity for travel per trip. As a disadvantage can be considered the slowness and the need for other means of transport to bring the merchandise to it.

Inflatable boats - Bote inflable

yachts - yate

Air Transport

This type of transport is made up of vehicles capable of moving by air. It commonly carries passengers along with merchandise.
This type of transport is made up of vehicles capable of moving by air. It commonly carries passengers along with merchandise. This type of transport offers an important advantage due to its speed. For this reason, the cost of insurance is cheaper. It also has a great availability of flights. However, it is the means of transport with the least transport capacity. Also, transportation costs are higher.
